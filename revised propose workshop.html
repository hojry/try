INTRODUCTION

Nutrition education can be described as any set of learning opportunities intended to promote the voluntary implementation of eating and other dietary behaviors conducive to health and well-being. It is an important aspect of delivering nutrition services to people. The purpose of nutrition education is to improve particular nutrition-related activities or behaviors to modify patterns that lead to bad health; this is achieved by providing a desire for change among people, by developing desirable food and dietary behaviors to encourage and protect good health. Indirectly, completing nutrition education will make one's life easier. Victual Workshop will be all about us showing our vision about having a good nutrition education to the targeted audience making them more knowledgeable in healthy dietary aspects in life. .

WHY IS THIS WORKSHOP IMPORTANT?

Nutrition education is one of the renowned problems that has been faced every now and then. Researchers from around the world have been assessing individuals if they are having good nutrition education or good nutrition itself to make good teachings of healthy dietary education. This workshop promotes nutrition education that is a necessary knowledge for everyone in view of the fact that it promotes positive outcomes, one is a good lifestyle choice as well as enhancing the participants critical thinking skills to be applied during their training. In ways the education system teaches students about subject matters is also the manner in which the Victual Workshop can teach its apprentices healthy nutritional awareness. Most people know that proper diet and physical exercise will help sustain a healthier weight. However, the benefits of a proper diet go beyond weights. Many diseases and conditions arise from poor health management but having good nutrition slows the disease progression, in light of this, people should have the right and knowledge in the fields of nutrition. The workshop will strive to assist its participants to promote that nutritional knowledge is important and must be viewed as such.


WHO SHOULD ATTEND THIS WORKSHOP?

Victual workshop will serve as an educator to those people who are in need of knowledge in the field of nutrition and those who don’t but is eager to gain knowledge in nutrition for it aims to raise awareness in valuing healthy dietary education. The workshop will provide both mentor and mentee to explore points, mastery, and apprehension when it comes to nutrition. In addition to that, tips and fitness lifestyles will also be discussed with modules and handouts provided. In later days, the training and learnings of the participants will be beneficial to their life as healthy and fit individuals.


WHAT PARTICIPANTS WILL LEARN DURING WORKSHOP?

After a productive workshop, participants will discover how exciting learning about differentconcepts and systems about good nutrition can be, but most importantly, they will have their critical thinking skills developed and will immediately set them apart from the crowd. In addition,the participant will be able to take out what they have learnt from their colleagues and pass onthat experience.

OUTLINE FOR INTERACTIVE LEARNING ON GOOD NUTRITION:


I. WORKSHOP OVERVIEW AND ORIENTATION:

This orientation involves a participatory exercise to incorporate the idea of good nutrition education and to strengthen the bond between the mentor and it’s mentee.


II. COLLECTIVE RESEARCH:

How does good nutrition knowledge be essential in everyday lives?

The workshop will provide both mentor and mentee handouts which includes why having a good education on the field of nutrition is a must, how good nutrition education will benefit their lives and what they can do to secure a healthy diet.


III. PHASE 1: Asessment

How does critical thinking be sensible in nutrition education?

The workshop will enhance the participants' critical thinking by being aware and sensitive in all the things, particularly with the food and drinks that’s being taken, the community they live in , etc. That being said, it takes a big part in nutrition education as it makes every individual decide and think better on ways on how to improve and develop not only nourishment needs, but on how it will make a greater impact on one’s way of life as well.


VI. PHASE 2: Designing a collaborative Learning Process:

Collaborative learning is the educational method to use individuals to improve learning while collaborating together. This approach deliberately engages learners to process and synthesize knowledge and ideas, rather than to use memorizing facts of statistics and figures.

By this time, the participants must now be aware of the basics about good nutrition and incorporate their skills in critical thinking to devise a plan about achieving a healthy and fit for life.


V. PRACTICE THREE RULES OF CRITICAL THINKING:

Critical thinking requires the principled implementation of standards and practices in theconsideration of realistic and theoretical alternatives for the purpose of drawing decisions on those alternatives.

FUNDAMENTALS IN CRITICAL THINKING:

Deliberation

Evaluation

Application

Critical thinking is important because it helps learners to create logical conclusions. If a student actually wishes to learn more, then must consider the content and, in order to understand it, alternative solutions and problem-solving are a must.

Brainstorm ideas and strive to make it in actions through problem-solving exercises aimed at achieving concrete results.
